Welcome to the Domain Connection Wizard

This wizard will guide you through setting up and connecting your five Unstoppable Domains for the 918 Technologies BlockChain BailBonds system.

Your Unstoppable Domains:

  • Nine1Eight.blockchain (Primary Domain - Contract Engine)
  • Nine1Eight.crypto (Payment Processing)
  • Nine1Eight.nft (Contract Verification)
  • Nine1Eight.wallet (Wallet Integration)
  • Nine1Eight.x (Bondsman Network)

What This Wizard Will Help You Do:

  1. Set up your Unstoppable Domains with the correct content records
  2. Connect the domains to create a unified bail bonds system
  3. Configure cross-domain communication and authentication
  4. Verify all connections are working properly
  5. Test the system with a simulated bail bond scenario

Requirements:

  • You must own all five Unstoppable Domains
  • You need access to manage your domain settings
  • A Web3 wallet (MetaMask, Trust Wallet, etc.) is required
  • Basic understanding of blockchain technology
